Mini pumpkin burgers

Ingredients

  • Beyond meat burgers
  • Ready to roll pizza dough
  • BBQ sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Shredded cheese
  • Red onions

Preparation

  1. Split the burgers in desired sizes and fry until done
  2. Caramelise red onions by slowly frying them on sunflower oil
  3. Roll out the pizza dough and split the dough in sizes slightly bigger than the burger patties you fried
  4. Mix tomato paste and BBQ sauce to create the burger sauce
  5. Assemble the burgers by adding the sauce, burger, cheese and onions
  6. Wrap the burgers with strings to create pumpkin shapes
  7. Don't press the string tight, the dough will expand
  8. Let the burgers rest in the fridge until your oven is ready
  9. Mix some olive oil with turmeric to add color and "paint" your burgers
  10. Bake at 180*C for 30-45 minutes until nice and brown
  11. Before serving, add pretzel sticks for stems
