Graveyard chocolate mousse

Ingredients

  • Burbon biscuits
  • Oreo cookies
  • Lidl colored peanuts
  • Lidl gummy worms
  • 300g Chocolate
  • 2 packs of silken tofu
  • White fondaint for writing

Preparation

  1. Mix the silken tofu with melted chocolate
  2. Add to small serving ramekins or cups
  3. Refridgerate for a few hours or overnight
  4. Decorate with crumbled cookies, peanuts and worms
  5. Write "RIP" on burbon biscuits and decorate
