Roasted Carrot Dip

Ingredients

  • As many carrots as you like
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tahini
  • Lemons
  • Sesame seeds and/or pine nuts to serve

Preparation

  1. Chop and roast the carrots on 180*C with some olive oil, salt and pepper, until nicely browned, approx 30-40 min
  2. Once carrots are cooled, try to resist eating them before continuing
  3. Blend all the ingredients with some extra olive oil until you get your desired consistency and flavour
  4. Refridgerate until ready to serve
  5. Sprinkle with some sesame seeds and roasted pine nuts
  6. Serve with your favourite veggies or spread on crackers
