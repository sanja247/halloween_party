Festive fruit platter

Ingredients

  1. Mango and blacberry skewers
  2. Monster apples
  3. Strawberry ghosts
  4. Satsuma pumpkins

Preparation

  1. Wash, chop and put them on skewers
  2. Cut the apples, soak in salt/lemon solution, add peanut butter, marhmallows and strawberry slice
  3. Dip the strawberried in white chocolate and draw eyes
  4. Dress the satsumas to look like pumpkins :)
