Pumpkin cheese dip

Ingredients

  • 3 packs vegan cream cheese (I used the Tesco brand)
  • 200 g vegan cheddar style cheese
  • Handful of fresh chives
  • Plastic foil to wrap into shape
  • Rubber bands to shape pumpkin
  • Stem of a bell pepper for decoration
  • To serve: nachos and crackers

Preparation

  1. Mix the cream cheese with chopped chives and a handful of cheddar cheese
  2. At this point you can add anything you like, like herbs, jalapenos, gurkins etc.
  3. Use the rest of the cheddar cheese to cover the cheese ball
  4. Wrap the ball in plastic foil and wrap with 4 rubber bands to shape a pumpkin
  5. Refridgerate until firm, approx 1 hour or more
  6. Unwrap the foil, add the bell pepper stem and serve with your favorite crackers and nachos
